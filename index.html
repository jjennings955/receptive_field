Receptive Field

Instructions: Hover over a 'pixel' (output) in the last layer (final canvas) to view the receptive field for that pixel. This is the outputs of the previous layers that this pixel 'can see' (is a function of, depends on).

Note: Each layer output shape is 16x16, and we're assuming a filter size of 3x3, strides of 1x1, padding='same'
